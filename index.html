Low cost, low power (at max speed 24W), high airflow fan

Here we are making a fan using a 12V motor and propeller blade. This fan consumes only 24W at full speed. The air flow is very high so that we cannot hold our face for a long time against it. We are fixing the motor in an aluminium frame. The motor we used is RS-775 which has very high RPM and torque. The link for buying the things for making this fan is give to the description of the video. The speed of the fan is controlled by circuit built using LM-350 regulator. Circuit diagram is also shown in the video. Anybody can built the circuit very easily.
