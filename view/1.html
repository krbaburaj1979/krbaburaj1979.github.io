March 11, 2018

In this video we are repairing the faulty micro USB mobile charger. A person with average knowledge in soldering wires and components can do this within 10 minutes. Not only micro USB in mobile charger, but also any USB connectors can be repaired with the help of this video. While soldering DO NOT keep soldering iron's tip into the micro USB connector for a long time.

The tools we are using for this operation are cutting plier, knife, soldering iron, lead, flux, glue gun and multimeter.
